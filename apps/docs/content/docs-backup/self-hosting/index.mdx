# Getting Started with Self-Hosting

This is a step-by-step guide to setting up and hosting your own Documenso instance. Before getting started, [select the right license for you](/docs/users/licenses).

## Why Self-Host?

- **Data Sovereignty** - Keep all your data on your own infrastructure
- **Customization** - Full control over configuration and branding
- **Compliance** - Meet specific regulatory requirements
- **Cost Control** - Scale according to your needs

## Before You Begin

Before deploying Documenso, make sure you understand:

1. **[Licensing](/docs/users/licenses)** - Choose the right license for your use case
2. **System Requirements** - Ensure your infrastructure meets the minimum requirements
3. **Environment Variables** - Prepare your configuration

## Minimum Requirements

- **CPU**: 1 vCPU
- **RAM**: 2GB
- **Storage**: 10GB
- **Database**: PostgreSQL 14+
- **Node.js**: 18+ (for source builds)
