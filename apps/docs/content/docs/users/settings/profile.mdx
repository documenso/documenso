## Avatar

Your avatar appears on documents you send and in your public profile.

{/* prettier-ignore */}
<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** > **Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Upload Avatar** next to your current avatar and select an image (PNG or JPG, max 1MB).
  </Step>
</Steps>

To remove your avatar, hover over the image and click **Remove**.

## Name

Your name appears on documents and email notifications sent to recipients.

{/* prettier-ignore */}
<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** > **Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Update the **Full Name** field and click **Update profile**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email

Your email address is shown as read-only on the profile page. To change your email address, contact support.

## Signature

Your signature is used as the default when you sign documents. You can create your signature using three methods:

{/* prettier-ignore */}
<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** > **Profile** and click on the **Signature** field to open the signature editor.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose one of three methods:

    | Method     | Description                                                |
    | ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
    | **Draw**   | Use your mouse or touchscreen to draw your signature       |
    | **Type**   | Enter your name and select from generated signature styles |
    | **Upload** | Upload an image of your signature                          |

  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Next** to confirm your signature, then click **Update profile** to save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  When signing documents, you can create a different signature for that specific document without
  changing your default signature.
</Callout>
