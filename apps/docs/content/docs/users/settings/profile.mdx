## Update Your Profile Information

### Avatar

Your avatar appears on documents you send and in your public profile.

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open profile settings

Go to **Settings** > **Profile**

</Step>
<Step>
### Upload avatar

Click **Upload Avatar** next to your current avatar and select an image (PNG or JPG, max 1MB).

</Step>
</Steps>

To remove your avatar, hover over the image and click **Remove**.

### Name

Your name appears on documents and email notifications sent to recipients.

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open profile settings

Go to **Settings** > **Profile**

</Step>
<Step>
### Update name

Update the **Full Name** field and click **Update profile**

</Step>
</Steps>

### Email

Your email address is shown as read-only on the profile page. To change your email address, contact support.

## Manage Your Signature

Your signature is used as the default when you sign documents. You can create your signature using three methods:

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open the signature editor

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**
2. Click on the **Signature** field to open the signature editor

</Step>

<Step>
### Create your signature

Choose one of three methods:

| Method     | Description                                                |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Draw**   | Use your mouse or touchscreen to draw your signature       |
| **Type**   | Enter your name and select from generated signature styles |
| **Upload** | Upload an image of your signature                          |

</Step>

<Step>
### Save your signature

Click **Next** to confirm your signature, then click **Update profile** to save.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  When signing documents, you can create a different signature for that specific document without
  changing your default signature.
</Callout>

## Configure Your Public Profile

Your public profile allows others to view your information and access templates you share publicly. Your profile is accessible at:

```
https://app.documenso.com/p/<your-username>
```

![Enable public profile](/public-profile/documenso-enable-public-profile-settings.webp)

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open public profile settings

Go to **Settings** > **Public Profile**

</Step>
<Step>
### Control visibility

Toggle **Show/Hide** to control profile visibility.

</Step>
<Step>
### Set URL and bio

Set your **Public profile URL** (personal accounts only) and add a **Bio** to describe yourself or your services.

</Step>
<Step>
### Save

Click **Update** to save.

</Step>
</Steps>

![Public profile settings](/public-profile/documenso-public-profile-settings.webp)

### Link Templates to Your Public Profile

Display templates on your public profile so visitors can sign documents directly:

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open public profile

Go to **Settings** > **Public Profile**

</Step>
<Step>
### Link template

Click **Link template** in the Templates section and select templates that have direct links enabled.

</Step>
<Step>
### Confirm

Linked templates appear on your public profile page.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Only templates with direct links enabled can be added to your public profile. See [Direct
  Links](/docs/users/documents/direct-links) to learn more.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">A free Documenso account gives you 5 free signatures per month.</Callout>

## Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data.

<Callout type="warn">
  Account deletion is irreversible. All documents, signatures, and templates will be permanently
  removed. Any active subscription will be cancelled.
</Callout>

To delete your account:

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open profile

Go to **Settings** > **Profile** and scroll to the **Delete Account** section.

</Step>
<Step>
### Delete account

Click **Delete Account**.

</Step>
<Step>
### Confirm

Type your email address to confirm and click **Confirm Deletion**.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  If you have two-factor authentication enabled, you must disable it before deleting your account.
</Callout>
