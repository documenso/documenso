## Prerequisites

- An organization.
  - Teams exist within an organizations.
- Organization Admin or Manager role to create teams.

## Limitations

- **Team name**: 3-30 characters
- **Team URL**: 3-30 characters, lowercase letters, numbers, dashes, and underscores only
- **Team URL** cannot start or end with dashes or underscores
- **Team URL** cannot contain consecutive dashes or underscores
- Maximum number of teams depends on your plan

## Create a Team

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open the create team dialog

1. Go to your organization page
2. Click **Create team**

![Team creation button on the organization page](/teams/team-create.webp)

</Step>

<Step>
### Enter team details

Fill in the required information:

- **Team Name** - A display name for your team (3-30 characters)
- **Team URL** - A unique identifier used in URLs like `app.documenso.com/t/your-team-url`

The Team URL is automatically generated from your team name, but you can customize it.

![Team creation dialog with name and URL fields](/teams/team-create-dialog.webp)

</Step>

<Step>
### Configure member access

Choose whether to allow all organization members to access this team:

<Tabs items={['Checked', 'Unchecked']}>
  <Tab value="Checked">All current and future organization members can access team documents.</Tab>
  <Tab value="Unchecked">
    Only organization admins and managers are added initially; you control member access manually.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">You can change member access settings later from team settings.</Callout>

</Step>

<Step>
### Create the team

Click **Create Team** to finish. You'll be taken to your new team's workspace.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Team Settings

After creating a team, you can configure additional settings from **Team Settings**:

<Tabs items={['General', 'Branding', 'Other']}>
  <Tab value="General">
    - **Team Name** - Update the display name
    - **Team URL** - Change the URL identifier (this changes all team links)
    - **Team Avatar** - Upload an image to identify your team
    - **Team Email** - Add an email address for your team (see [Team Email](#team-email) below)
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Branding">
    Customize how your team's documents appear to recipients:

    - **Enable Custom Branding** - Turn branding on/off, or inherit from organization settings
    - **Branding Logo** - Upload your logo (max 5MB, JPG, PNG, or WebP) to display on signing pages
    - **Brand Website** - Link your logo to your website
    - **Brand Details** - Additional information shown at the bottom of emails

    <Callout type="info">
      Team branding settings can inherit from your organization. Select "Inherit from organization" to
      use organization-level branding, or configure team-specific branding.
    </Callout>

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Other">
    - **Document Preferences** - Default settings for new documents
    - **Email Preferences** - Customize email notifications
    - **Public Profile** - Configure your team's public presence
    - **API Tokens** - Generate tokens for API access
    - **Webhooks** - Set up integrations with external services
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

![Document visibility settings in team preferences](/teams/document-visibility-settings.webp)

## Team Email

Adding a team email address enables several capabilities:

<Tabs items={['Team Inbox', 'Send on behalf', 'Track documents', 'Consistent branding']}>
  <Tab value="Team Inbox">View signing requests sent to this email address.</Tab>
  <Tab value="Send on behalf">
    Documents sent from the team appear to come from the team email rather than your personal email.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Track documents">See documents sent from this email in the team's document list.</Tab>
  <Tab value="Consistent branding">
    Recipients see the team identity rather than individual sender details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

To configure a team email:

1. Go to **Team Settings** > **General**
2. Enter the email address in the **Team Email** field
3. Click **Update** to save

<Callout type="info">
  The team email is used as the sender identity for documents sent from the team. Recipients will
  see this email address in signing notifications.
</Callout>
