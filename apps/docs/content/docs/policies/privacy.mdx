This page summarizes how Documenso collects, uses, and protects your data. For the full legal terms, see the [Privacy Policy](https://documenso.com/privacy) on the main website.

## Data Collected

When you use Documenso Cloud, the following data is collected:

### Account Information

- Email address
- Name
- Password (hashed)
- Profile settings

### Documents and Signatures

- Uploaded PDF documents
- Signature images and data
- Recipient information (names and email addresses)
- Signing timestamps and IP addresses
- Audit trail data

### Usage Data

- Authentication logs
- API access logs
- Feature usage patterns
- Error reports

### Technical Data

- IP addresses
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Cookies and session data

## How Data Is Used

Documenso uses your data to:

- **Provide the service**: Store and process documents, deliver signing requests, generate audit trails
- **Authenticate users**: Verify identity during sign-in and document signing
- **Maintain security**: Detect unauthorized access, prevent abuse, investigate incidents
- **Improve the product**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
- **Communicate**: Send transactional emails (signing requests, completions), service announcements, and optional marketing (with consent)

Documenso does not sell your personal data.

## Data Retention

| Data Type           | Retention Period                          |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Account data        | Until account deletion                    |
| Completed documents | Until deleted by user or account deletion |
| Audit trails        | Retained with associated documents        |
| Authentication logs | 90 days                                   |
| Deleted documents   | Permanently removed within 30 days        |

When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed. This process is irreversible.

## Third-Party Services

Documenso Cloud uses the following third-party services:

| Service                | Purpose                                    |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Cloud hosting provider | Infrastructure and data storage            |
| Email delivery service | Sending signing requests and notifications |
| Payment processor      | Subscription billing                       |
| Analytics              | Usage metrics (anonymized)                 |

All third-party providers are selected based on their security practices and compliance with data protection regulations. Data processing agreements are in place where required.

<Callout type="info">
  The specific providers used may change. The current list is available in the full [Privacy
  Policy](https://documenso.com/privacy).
</Callout>

## Self-Hosted Privacy

When you self-host Documenso:

- **Your data stays with you**: All documents, signatures, and user data remain on your infrastructure
- **No data sent to Documenso**: The self-hosted application does not phone home or send telemetry to Documenso servers
- **You control retention**: Define your own data retention policies
- **You choose providers**: Select your own database, storage, and email providers

<Callout type="warn">
  Self-hosted deployments are subject to your own privacy policy. You are responsible for compliance
  with applicable data protection laws.
</Callout>

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request removal of your data
- **Portability**: Export your data in a standard format
- **Restriction**: Limit how your data is processed
- **Objection**: Opt out of certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@documenso.com or delete your account directly in the application settings.

### Data Export

You can export your documents at any time:

1. Go to **Documents**
2. Open a document
3. Click **Download** to save the signed PDF

Account data can be requested by contacting support.

### Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Profile**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm the deletion

See [Security Settings](/docs/users/settings/security#delete-your-account) for detailed instructions.
