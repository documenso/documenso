MADVERSE DISTRIBUTION AGREEMENT (ENTERPRISE PLAN)

This Distribution Agreement (hereinafter referred to as "Agreement") is entered into and effective as of [Date], (the "Effective Date"), by and between Nesho Entertainment Private Limited, with registered address at Golf View Corporate Tower, Sector 42, Gurugram, Haryana 122002, India (hereinafter referred to as "MADverse") and Compass Box Studio, with registered address at 307, Off, Airport Rd, behind

MADverse and the Label shall individually be referred to as Party and collectively as Parties.


Whereas, MADverse is a service provider in the music industry engaged in providing artist related services such as procurement, distribution and dissemination of audio and audio-visual content using existing and emerging technologies and distribution platforms now known or devised in future throughout the world including, but not limited to, various digital platforms as a music distributor of sound recordings.


Whereas, the Label is the owner of or retains control over certain Content (as defined in Schedule A and as specifically designated by the Label via functionality provided on the MADverse platform) and is desirous of exploiting, promoting, distributing, monetizing and publishing the Content. In this regard, the Label intends to appoint MADverse as its exclusive partner to exclusively undertake the aforesaid activities.


Unless otherwise defined in the body of this Agreement, all capitalised terms used in this Agreement shall be set out in Schedule A.

    1.     2. The Label further grants to MADverse the right to exploit and/or distribute the entire copyright in the Content and collect all revenue deriving from the distribution of the Content in the manner specified herein, through all Distribution Channels (or such Distribution Channels as may be specifically designated by you, via functionality provided on the MADverse platform).

    3. MADverse shall exclusively be entitled to distribute, publish, or otherwise exploit commercially, the Content and/or any part thereof to the public for the Term of the Agreement. Accordingly, the Label cannot make or have the same Content available to any Distribution Channels using any service other than the MADverse platform for the Term of the Agreement.

    4. The Label shall, as on the Effective Date of this Agreement, migrate any and all of the Content, to the MADverse platform in the manner as may be specified by MADverse to the Label (hereinafter the "Migration Process").

    5. MADverse shall have the exclusive right to sell, distribute and package the Content in any way it deems fit, including without limitation by including the Content in a compilation.

    6. This license does not transfer or assign any ownership rights in the Content to MADverse. All rights not expressly granted herein are reserved by the Label.

  2.    ​ PAYMENT OF NET RECEIPTS


    1. In consideration of the distribution services provided hereunder, MADverse shall retain 15% (fifteen percent) of the Net Receipts (defined below) (the "MADverse Enterprise Plan"). Under the MADverse Enterprise Plan, the Label shall be entitled to distribute the Content via MADverse, in the manner specified under Clause 1, for unlimited artists.

    2. MADverse shall pay the Label all the Net Receipts after deducting its share in the manner specified herein. For purposes of this Agreement, "Net Receipts" means the amounts MADverse actually receives and retains from Distribution Channels (including, without limitation, digital distribution solution providers), solely for the distribution of Content in the manner specified under Clause II, less any taxes, processing or transaction fees and other charges (including without limitation any charges made by a Distribution Channel or any third party service provider including, without limitation, digital distribution solution providers), file storage costs, mechanical royalty fees, transaction costs, reformatting costs if your Content, as applicable, are not provided in the format required by MADverse or such Distribution Channel, returns, credits or deductions made due to technically faulty, defective or unfit Content or fraudulent activity by end users, and legal, accounting and other professional fees incurred by MADverse in relation to any Agreement, claim or dispute with a licensee or third party service provider related thereto.

    3. MADverse shall not be responsible for providing percentages of Net Receipts to any additional third party participants in the Content.

    4. MADverse shall be entitled to deduct or set off any charges or additional amounts for any additional features and services accessed which are not provided hereunder. Any and all subscription fees and opt-in feature fees are non-refundable and not on a pro-rata basis.

    5. All Net Receipts and other charges required to be paid or remitted hereunder are exclusive of all taxes imposed or payable, whether now or in the future, in connection with this Agreement, all of which shall be borne by the Label.

    6. The Label shall be solely responsible and liable to pay any royalties, fees and other monies that are or may become payable to the authors, composers, artists, producers, publishers, administrators and other holders of rights related to any and all of the Content.

    3. Either Party shall have the right to terminate this Agreement forthwith at any time during the Term of this Agreement on providing prior written notice of at least sixty (60) days to the other Party.

    4. The Agreement may be terminated by either Party in the event of occurrence of a force majeure event for a continuous period of more than sixty (60) days.

    5. Consequences of Termination: Upon termination of this Agreement in the manner specified herein and upon the receipt of such written request from the Label, MADverse shall takedown all or any Content, as specified, from the MADverse platform and the Distribution Channels.
